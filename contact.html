Frequently Asked

We recommend booking at least 6–12 months in advance for peak season (July–October), especially if gorilla trekking is on your itinerary — permits are limited and sell out fast. Shoulder and low seasons can be arranged in 4–8 weeks, though earlier is always better for the best lodge availability.
Yes — Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Uganda, and Zanzibar are all well-established safari destinations with excellent tourism infrastructure. National parks and private conservancies are some of the safest environments in Africa. We monitor conditions continuously and our guides are trained in first aid and emergency protocols.
Our packages include all in-country transport, accommodation, meals as specified, park fees, guide fees, and any permit costs. International flights are not included (we can arrange these separately). Each package listing shows exactly what is and isn't included — nothing is hidden.
Absolutely. Every package can be extended, shortened, combined with another destination, or adjusted to match your preferred lodging tier and activities. Just let us know what you'd like to change in your enquiry — we'll send a revised quote within 48 hours.
We offer full refunds for cancellations made more than 60 days before departure, minus a 10% administration fee. Between 30–60 days, a 50% refund applies. Under 30 days is non-refundable, though we strongly recommend travel insurance which covers most circumstances. See our full Terms & Conditions.
Yes — we have dedicated family safari packages with child-friendly guides, age-appropriate activities, and lodges with family accommodation. Most parks recommend a minimum age of 6 for game drives. Gorilla trekking requires a minimum age of 15. We'll flag any age restrictions clearly when designing your itinerary.
Requirements vary by nationality and destination. Generally, yellow fever vaccination is required for Uganda and Rwanda and recommended for all destinations. Most nationalities can get a visa on arrival or e-visa for Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and Rwanda. We provide a detailed pre-departure health and visa checklist with every booking.
