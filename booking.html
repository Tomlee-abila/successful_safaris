Common Questions
What's included in the package price? ▾
Accommodation, all meals, house wines & drinks, game drives, park fees, air charter from Nairobi, and a dedicated guide. International flights are not included unless specified.
What is the cancellation policy? ▾
Free cancellation up to 90 days before departure. 50% refund 60–89 days. 25% refund 30–59 days. No refund within 30 days. We strongly recommend travel insurance.
Do I need vaccinations for Kenya? ▾
Yellow fever vaccination is required if travelling from certain countries. Malaria prophylaxis is strongly recommended. Our health kit add-on includes everything you need.
Can I customise the itinerary further? ▾
Absolutely. After booking, a dedicated safari specialist will contact you within 24 hours to personalise every detail of your journey.
