Package Overview

The Masai Mara at its most electric

The Great Migration is not one event — it is a continuous story of survival, abundance, and predation that unfolds across 365 days of the Mara calendar. Our seven-night itinerary positions you in the heart of the action during the peak crossing season, with private game vehicles, KPSGA-certified guides, and intimate bush camps that place you inside the wilderness rather than alongside it.

This is the definitive Kenya safari — crafted over sixteen years of intimate Mara knowledge. We operate only with camps that hold exclusive conservancy access, giving you game areas inaccessible to standard park vehicles, and the freedom to drive off-road, stay out after dark, and experience the Mara as few ever have.

Migration River Crossings
+

Two full days focused on the Mara River — the most dramatic amphitheatre in all of nature. We spend full days at the river, moving between points as animal behaviour dictates.
